About Us

Ayyappa Construction is a trusted hometown builder based in Kilari Nagar, Vizianagaram, led by proprietor Kilari Nagaraju. Operating since 1995 in both Vizianagaram and Visakhapatnam, we specialize in residential layouts, apartments, and commercial construction with a strong focus on quality, transparency, and long-term value. With over two decades of experience, we have built a reputation for excellence and customer satisfaction across Andhra Pradesh.
